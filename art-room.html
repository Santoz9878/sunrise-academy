About Our Art Room

Our creative art room is a vibrant space where students explore their artistic talents and express their imagination. Equipped with various art supplies and materials, the room fosters creativity, fine motor skills, and artistic appreciation.

🎨 Creative Expression

Students develop artistic skills through painting, drawing, and sculpture, learning to express emotions and ideas visually.

👐 Fine Motor Skills

Art activities strengthen hand-eye coordination and fine motor development essential for writing and other tasks.

🧠 Cognitive Development

Creative activities stimulate imagination, problem-solving, and spatial reasoning abilities.

💝 Emotional Growth

Art therapy and self-expression help students process emotions and build self-confidence.
