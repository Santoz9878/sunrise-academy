About Our Computer Laboratory

Our modern computer laboratory is equipped with the latest technology to prepare students for the digital age. With high-speed internet, educational software, and programming tools, the lab supports digital literacy and technological proficiency across all grade levels.

💻 Digital Literacy

Students master essential computer skills including typing, file management, and internet safety.

🎯 Coding Skills

Introduction to programming languages and computational thinking prepares students for future careers in technology.

🔍 Research Tools

Access to online databases, educational websites, and research tools enhances learning across all subjects.

🎨 Digital Creativity

Graphic design, video editing, and multimedia tools allow students to express creativity digitally.
