About Our Library

Our modern library serves as the heart of academic excellence at Sunrise Academy. With extensive collections of books, digital resources, and quiet study spaces, the library plays a crucial role in fostering a love for learning and supporting academic achievement.

📚 Academic Support

Students have access to textbooks, reference materials, and research resources that complement classroom learning and support homework assignments.

🧠 Critical Thinking

The library encourages independent research, critical thinking, and information literacy skills essential for success in the CBC curriculum.

📖 Reading Culture

We promote a reading culture through story time, book clubs, and recreational reading areas that help develop vocabulary and comprehension skills.

💻 Digital Learning

Modern computer stations provide access to educational websites, online databases, and digital learning tools for comprehensive research.
