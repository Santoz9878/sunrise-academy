About Our Science Laboratory

Our state-of-the-art science laboratory is equipped with modern facilities and equipment to provide hands-on learning experiences. The lab supports the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum by enabling students to conduct experiments, observe phenomena, and develop scientific inquiry skills.

🔬 Hands-on Experiments

Students conduct real experiments in physics, chemistry, and biology, applying theoretical knowledge to practical situations.

🧪 Scientific Inquiry

The lab fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills through guided scientific investigations.

📊 Data Analysis

Students learn to collect, analyze, and interpret experimental data, developing essential research skills.

🌱 Environmental Awareness

Through ecology and environmental science activities, students develop awareness of sustainability and conservation.
